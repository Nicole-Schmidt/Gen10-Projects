Restaurant Description

Niki's Cafe serves up all your favorite food with that special twist. The restaurant was opened in 2014 by Niki Schmidt after she decided that the world needed to try her food.

Niki has won multiple awards for her food. Most recently the "Best Homemade BBQ" at the 2018 Twin Cities Condiment Connoisseur Convention.
